# Citation Spec

Research-backed documents must include citations or a Sources section. Public
factual claims must be traceable through evidence to normalized source records.

Short quotations require a source ID and a human-readable locator. FounderOS
research records limit stored quotations to 25 words. Paraphrases must preserve
the source's meaning and clearly separate source opinion from FounderOS synthesis.

Generated research briefs use stable source identifiers such as `SRC-001` so a
reader can resolve every claim against the Sources section.
