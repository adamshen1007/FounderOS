# Beginner Beta Protocol — Volume 1

Status: Ready; no sessions recorded

## Purpose

Determine whether a representative first-time founder can use one chapter to
make a concrete decision without private coaching from the author.

## Minimum Preview Evidence

- At least three representative readers
- At least one completed chapter worksheet per reader
- Sanitized notes about comprehension, confusion, and the next action
- A resolution decision for every blocking finding

This is a content review. It is separate from the FounderOS platform pilot.

## Session Steps

1. Assign a participant code and explain how notes will be used.
2. Ask the reader to choose a current founder decision without sharing secrets.
3. Ask the reader to select and read the relevant chapter.
4. Ask the reader to complete the worksheet independently.
5. Ask the reader to explain the decision and next action in their own words.
6. Record confusion, skipped sections, time taken, and requested clarification.
7. Remove names, company secrets, customer data, and sensitive financial data.
8. Classify each finding as blocking, important, or optional.

## Completion Questions

- Could the reader identify the correct chapter?
- Could the reader distinguish evidence from an assumption?
- Did the worksheet create a decision rather than more notes?
- Did the reader identify a responsible next action?
- Did any passage imply a guarantee, endorsement, or professional advice?
