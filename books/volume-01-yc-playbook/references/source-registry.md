# Source Registry

This registry is the canonical source list for Volume 1. It records primary and
first-party material that may support chapter claims. Inclusion does not mean a
source endorses FounderOS or that every statement in it is adopted.

Review date: 2026-07-16

## Source Classes

- **Primary guidance:** the author or institution states its own framework,
  advice, policy, or experience.
- **Official standard:** a public body publishes a framework or technical
  reference.
- **FounderOS synthesis:** a conclusion developed in the manuscript; it must be
  labeled as interpretation and should connect at least two identified sources.

## Use Rules

- Cite the source in the chapter’s Sources section and attach a footnote to the
  supported claim.
- Paraphrase by default. Keep any quotation within repository copyright limits.
- Do not convert historical examples into universal laws.
- Do not present current YC application, investment, or legal details unless
  reviewed again on the publication date.
- Route legal, financial, safety, or regulatory decisions to qualified review.
- Record new sources here before relying on them in a chapter.
