# FounderOS Constitution

## Mission

FounderOS exists to help founders repeatedly transform ideas into successful companies through structured knowledge, disciplined execution, and AI-assisted automation.

## Foundational Principles

1. Documentation before implementation.
2. Research before commitment.
3. Solve real founder problems.
4. Small, verifiable milestones over speculative efforts.
5. Human judgment remains accountable for strategic decisions.
6. Automation must be reproducible and observable.
7. Open standards are preferred over vendor lock-in.
8. Knowledge compounds through reuse.

## Canonical Sources

The source of truth is Git + Markdown. Published formats such as Notion pages, PDFs, DOCX files, EPUBs, and websites are outputs.

## AI Governance

AI agents may assist with research, drafting, coding, testing, documentation, and publishing, but strategic decisions require human approval.
