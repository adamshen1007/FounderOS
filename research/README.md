# Research

Store traceable research topics here. Each topic keeps its manifest, normalized
source metadata, evidence notes, classified claims, and generated brief together.

```text
research/topics/<topic>/
├── research.yaml
├── sources/
├── evidence/
├── claims/
├── outputs/research-brief.md
└── .founderos/research-state.json
```

Do not store full copyrighted pages. Preserve canonical URLs, short quotations
when necessary, paraphrased notes, and human-readable locators. Follow the
[research automation guide](../docs/05-operations/research-automation.md).
